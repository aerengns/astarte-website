Agriculture System of Tomato Alerting, Raising, Tracking, and Exhibiting

Our product is designed to solve the miscommunication and tracking/monitoring problems in tomato farms. Say goodbye to missed updates and misunderstandings between workers and owners. With ASTARTE, you can ensure everyone is on the same page.

Features

Real-Time Task Management

Assign tasks to workers and monitor their progress in real time and receive instant notifications when tasks are completed. Stay updated on the progress of field activities without manual follow-up, ensuring tasks are finished as scheduled.

Intelligent Irrigation and Fertilization Suggestions

Utilize weather and soil data to receive intelligent recommendations for irrigation and fertilization. The app analyzes real-time conditions and provides suggestions to optimize your water and nutrient management, maximizing the efficiency of your farming practices and ensuring healthy crop growth.

Risk Alerts and Disease Management

Get real-time alerts for potential weather related risks based on real-time data and access detailed information on diseases and pests to protect your crops. Additionally, connect with agricultural experts through the app to get expert opinions.

Soil Monitoring

Easily monitor and record essential soil metrics like moisture, pH levels, and NPK values while visualizing them through heatmaps. These records are tagged with GPS data, allowing you to precisely track and analyze soil conditions across your farm.

Historical Activity Tracking

Access a comprehensive history of farm activities and previously acquired data. This feature allows you to review past actions and track progress over time enabling data-driven decision-making in your farming practices.
