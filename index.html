How It Works

  1. Collecting Crucial Data
    Our app lets you collect and records vital soil data (moisture, PH, and NPK levels) via sensors. This data is tagged with GPS coordinates for precise location-specific analysis. Users can also upload photos of their farmland, providing visual insights.
  2. Data Visualization and Insights
    The app processes the collected data to provide visual representations such as heatmaps of soil conditions. It also provides data-driven suggestions for crucial tasks like irrigation and fertilization.
  3. Efficient Coordination
    We've incorporated a notification system that allows farm owners to assign tasks to their workers and get notified upon task completion. This ensures smooth operations and boosts efficiency.
  4. Weather Alerts and Disease Prevention
    The application integrates real-time weather data, acting as an early warning system that sends alerts to users about potential environmental risks. It also provides alerts on possible diseases and pest outbreaks, providing timely preventative measures.
  5. Data History
    Users have the ability to access a comprehensive history of their farming activities. This includes tasks, events, and previously collected data, all of which can be instrumental in facilitating informed decision-making for future operations.

Tech Stack

In the backend, we use Django, a high-level Python web framework known for its simplicity and practicality. The frontend is built using Flutter and Firebase, which are renowned for their performance and ease of use. Docker helps us encapsulate our app in containers, ensuring its smooth operation across different computing environments. Lastly, we use GitLab for version control, supporting our collaborative development efforts.
